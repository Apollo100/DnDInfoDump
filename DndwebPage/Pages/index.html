The Story So Far

A Quick Recap of The DnD Campaign

Our adventurers began there journey in the wonderful town of EndTown, a small town of roughly 2,000 people that found it's stride in the paint industry. Upon arrival in town, the group made their way to the pub, The Drunken Fighter, where they were greated by a kind group of locals that consisted of; Kirk, Grand Paladin Roman, and Kirks Kid's (three beautiful half-human/half-dragonborne). It was in this tavern they found the job board and decided that they would begin working to make lasting mark on this town. They looked over the available jobs, and carefully accepted one that seemed easy enogh - a farm has been being terrorized through the night, someone was destroying their crops and they needed someone to watch over them through the night. Upon arriving at the farm they were greeted by an old raggedy farmer who introduced himself as Oldman Felix McFee.
